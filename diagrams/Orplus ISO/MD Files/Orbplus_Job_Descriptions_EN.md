## 1. Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

**Position:** Chief Executive Officer
**Department:** Executive
**Reports to:** Board of Directors
**Current Holder:** K.Kirk

### Job Summary
The CEO is responsible for overall strategic direction, business development, and day-to-day operations of Orbplus. This role combines executive leadership with hands-on involvement in sales and product development.

### Key Responsibilities
- Define and execute company vision, mission, and strategic goals
- Lead business development and customer acquisition efforts
- Oversee all departments and ensure alignment with company objectives
- Manage relationships with key customers, partners, and stakeholders
- Make high-level decisions regarding policy, strategy, and operations
- Ensure financial health and sustainable growth of the company
- Represent the company in external communications and negotiations
- Build and maintain company culture and values

### Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Computer Science, or related field
- Minimum 10 years of experience in technology/software industry
- Proven track record in business development and leadership
- Strong understanding of SaaS business models
- Excellent communication and negotiation skills
- Strategic thinking and decision-making abilities

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Revenue growth and profitability
- Customer acquisition and retention rates
- Employee satisfaction and retention
- Strategic milestone achievement
