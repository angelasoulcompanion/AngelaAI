## üõ°Ô∏è Risk Management

### **1. Trading Risks**

**Market Risk:**
- **Risk:** Market conditions change, strategies stop working
- **Mitigation:**
  - Diversify across multiple strategies (momentum, mean reversion, ML-based)
  - Diversify across asset classes (stocks, crypto, forex)
  - Maximum 2-5% risk per trade
  - Stop-loss on every position
  - Maximum drawdown limit: 15%

**Execution Risk:**
- **Risk:** Slippage, failed orders, broker downtime
- **Mitigation:**
  - Use limit orders when possible
  - Multiple broker accounts (backup)
  - Real-time monitoring with alerts
  - Emergency stop button

**Model Risk:**
- **Risk:** Backtest overfitting, data snooping bias
- **Mitigation:**
  - Out-of-sample testing (walk-forward analysis)
  - Multiple validation periods
  - Conservative parameter selection
  - Regular strategy review every quarter

---

### **2. Business Risks**

**Revenue Concentration:**
- **Risk:** Too dependent on one revenue stream
- **Mitigation:**
  - 4-phase model = 4 revenue streams
  - By Year 2: No single stream >50% of revenue

**Regulatory Risk:**
- **Risk:** SEC/regulators require licenses for prop firm/SaaS
- **Mitigation:**
  - Consult lawyer before launching each phase
  - Budget ‡∏ø200K-500K for compliance
  - Structure business properly (multiple entities if needed)

**Technology Risk:**
- **Risk:** System failures, data loss, hacks
- **Mitigation:**
  - Daily database backups (automated)
  - Code version control (Git)
  - Security audits for SaaS platform
  - Bug bounty program

**Competition Risk:**
- **Risk:** Competitors copy business model
- **Mitigation:**
  - CQF expertise = defensible moat
  - Build brand early (thought leadership)
  - Continuous innovation (always 6-12 months ahead)
  - Network effects (SaaS community)
